This is my very first attempt at trying to write a blog. I have never really been much of a writer although jotting down my reflections has indeed become a much bigger part of my personal development process ever since attending my TGM course at INSEAD in 2017.

The experience in Fontainebleu was not only a thoroughly refreshing one for both m body and mind, but it had also served to re-ignite my core passion for people development. 

So on this day, I begin my first attempts at putting down my candid musings about leadership, management, communication and many other perennial hot topics that aspiring leaders and managers often have to contend with and hopefully this sharing will in turn benefit anyone who reads them.
